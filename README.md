# Sierra Atlantic Analytics Consulting Projects

## Overview
This repository contains analytics projects that simulate real-world consulting
engagements delivered for enterprise clients, modeled after typical Sierra Atlantic
client work. The projects demonstrate end-to-end analytics workflows, from exploratory
analysis to modeling and business recommendations.

Each client engagement is structured into three phases:
1. Exploratory Data Analysis
2. Modeling and Analytics
3. Business Insights and Recommendations

---

## Client Engagements

### 1. Retail Analytics Client
Focus: Customer behavior analysis, demand trends, and operational insights to
support inventory planning and marketing effectiveness.

### 2. Financial Services Client
Focus: Risk analysis and predictive modeling to support decision-making and
operational efficiency.
